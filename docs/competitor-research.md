## Recurring Patterns on Ranking Pages

1. High-urgency language in page title, H1, and first screen
2. 24/7 availability stated repeatedly
3. Above-the-fold click-to-call CTA
4. City + service keyword combinations in headings and metadata
5. Strong trust markers:
   - licensed and insured claims
   - years in business
   - review/rating signals
6. Service-area clusters (city pages and nearby areas)
7. FAQ or “what to do now” emergency guidance sections
8. Multiple conversion pathways (call + booking form)

## Falcon Five Scaffold Decisions Based on Findings

- Data-driven service pages and city pages to scale local SEO
- Dedicated emergency plumbing and AC pages as core pillars
- Sitewide call CTA and fast path to contact
- Built-in FAQ, breadcrumb, service, and local business schema
- Sitemap/robots configured for crawlability from day one
- Content hub scaffold for long-tail topical authority
